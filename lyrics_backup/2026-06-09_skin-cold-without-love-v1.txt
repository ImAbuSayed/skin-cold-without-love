[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The shadows are dancing across the wooden floor
I've never craved somebody this much before
The fire is fading into an ember's glow
I need to feel your touch before you have to go

[Verse 1]
Tracing down the highway just to see your face
Chasing down the moon to reach our hiding place
Your touch is like a blanket in the pouring rain
Washing off the miles and taking all the pain

[Pre-Chorus]
And every time you look at me, I'm falling right back in
I can feel the fever rising on my skin
You pull me a little closer, no space left in between
Waking up the passion of a midnight dream

[Chorus]
Because my skin is freezing when you pull away
I need your warm hands at the end of the day
It's a burning shiver that I never want to lose
My skin goes cold without the touch of you

[Post-Chorus]
[Echo] (Cold without your love)
Bring that fever back to me

[Verse 2]
Tangled in the covers while the crickets sing
I am wrapped around you like a fragile string
I just want the rhythm of your beating heart
Knowing that we never have to stay apart

[Echo] (Cold without your love)

[Bridge]
I trace the little secrets that the night withstood

Caught inside the silence of a midnight spell
Drinking from the water of a secret well
